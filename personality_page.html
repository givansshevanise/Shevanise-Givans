HOBBIES


Exploring multiple hobbies has profoundly impacted my academic life. Engaging in activities like doing puzzles and painting allows me to experience progress in small, tangible ways. With a heavy course load, I often find myself questioning if all the hard work is worth it. However, completing a puzzle or creating a painting reminds me that even small achievements matter. These hobbies give me hope and reinforce the idea that consistent effort leads to growth over time. I’ve seen significant improvement in my painting skills, illustrating how those little steps can culminate in meaningful progress, both in art and in my studies.


STRONGEST EMOTION

My strongest emotion is compassion. I’m the kind of person who instinctively offers help and support to others as soon as I sense they need it. I believe that often, people don’t require money or material things; they simply need someone to listen and provide genuine support. This trait has allowed me to be more down to earth with my group mates, helping me understand that we all have our own stories and battles to face every day. It has developed a level of patience I never knew I could have. I feel honored to be trusted with their stories—the ups and downs, and even the little wins they thought went unnoticed. Sharing my time to help and give in any way possible is a privilege that enriches my life and strengthens my connections with others.

STRONGEST TRAITS

One of my strongest traits is my ability to see the beauty in small things. Sometimes, the weight of our responsibilities feels overwhelming, making it seem impossible to continue. Yet, moments like watching clouds form or seeing the light dance on the surface of the water bring me joy and remind me of life’s simple pleasures. The way rain washes away the stress and pain of the day leaves me feeling calm and at peace, while the moon lights up even the darkest nights. This perspective not only enriches my daily life but also helps me academically, as it allows me to approach even the most challenging tasks with renewed clarity and optimism.
